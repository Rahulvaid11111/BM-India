White space is not empty — it is breathing room. It is confidence.

Design principles
1
Zero decoration
No gradients, no textures, no shadows. Structure and white space do all the work.
2
Type as image
Headlines carry visual weight. Large, spaced type is the photography of the page.
3
One column grid
Articles breathe at full width. No sidebars cluttering the reading experience.
4
Slow scroll
Content reveals with subtle fade-ins. Nothing rushes. The pace is intentional.
